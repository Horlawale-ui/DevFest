Attending in Person

As this is a hybrid event, we have some requirements for in-person attendees to ensure the safety of all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers.

All in-person attendees will be required to show proof of vaccination or a negative lateral flow test taken within the last 48 hours of the event starting.

Everyone will be required to wear a mask (unless you have a previous proof of exemption) for the duration of the event when inside and not eating or drinking.
